Major changes in v0.0.6 Alpha Part II


  • The layer IDs of the Exponent Coins has been changed from "p" to "ex". Any stuff that has the old layer ID would be changed to "ex".
  • The unused Poacher upgrades has now been removed.
  • Exponent Coins, Orbs, Poachers and Willy has been completely changed. Sectors are now finished
  • Rebalancing upgrades that can boost the game or just cause to inflate after moments when away.
  • Orbs effect is slightly increased and now affects the gain of Exponent Coins
  • Exponent coins no longer has a softcap upon reaching 1,000 of the gain but is pushed to 1e10 of them with a power of ^0.333. (not the currency amount)
  • All challenges have been completely changed and now they're slightly difficult
  • Dubnium and Hassium has been removed.
  • Carson Cookies and Cop Cookies were removed (probably because they have broken abillites)
  • Reincarnation is changed (now it gives a multipler to Poachers exponent, although it would be changed in a later update)
  • Added and removed most of the upgrades.
  • Added Exponent Coins milestones upon reaching 6 Orbs.
  • Added a softcap for Orbs upon reaching 40 of them.
  • Sectors now only needs 45 Orbs.
  • Poachers now only needs 38 Orbs.
  • Willy now only needs to complete 4 Orb Challenges
  • The endgame is balanced to 72,000 Sectors.
  • Added Repairments (layer) that are unlocked upon reaching 22,500 Sectors.
  • Added a new Poacher upgrade that is unlocked upon reaching 7,500 Superexponents
  • More changes are made to the game.
